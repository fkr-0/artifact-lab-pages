# Writing Guidelines

## Tone

Write like a capable peer who wants other people to become more capable too.

The voice should be:
- calm
- exact
- non-performative
- humane
- practical

## The new stylistic edge

The visual system may carry a slight handstyle / pixel / neon accent. The prose should **not**
become slang-heavy because of that. Keep language clean and adult. One sharp phrase is enough.

## Sentence shape

Prefer concrete nouns and verbs. Avoid hype verbs such as *revolutionize*, *dominate*, or
*weaponize*. Use direct invitations: *join*, *learn*, *test*, *compare*, *repair*, *share*.

## House line

**Learn quietly. Build carefully. Protect each other.**

## Approved vibe words

signal · field note · workshop · relay · clinic · patch · practice · safeguard · mutual · quiet
