# Naming and Subject Lines

## Naming pattern

Use:
**{project or program} — {artifact type} — {date or release}**

Examples:
- Device Clinic — workshop note — 2026-04
- Browser Safety — quick guide — r1
- Aster Relay — annual letter — 2026

## Email subjects

Keep them clean, readable, and specific.

Good:
- Aster Relay — workshop invite — browser safety clinic
- Field note — account recovery checklist
- Patch release — docs theme v1.1

Avoid:
- excessive punctuation
- forced cyber slang
- all-caps subject lines
- more than one metaphor in a title
