💣 Gridblast

Classic 2–4 player local Bomberman: square grid, solid posts, breakable blocks, timed bombs, cross explosions, chain reactions, and two upgrade axes.
Controls P1: WASD + Space P2: Arrows + Enter P3: IJKL + U P4: Numpad 8/4/5/6 + 0 Upgrades: 🔥 extends bomb cross, ＋ allows more active bombs.
