Font Lab Browser

Single-file, browser-only font builder. Upload a glyph grid image, remove white to transparency, smooth and trace, then export an OTF plus debug assets.

Browser limit: export and download only. System install/font-cache refresh must happen outside the browser.
Load an image and press “Process grid”.

Source

Mouse: drag selected cell to move override. Shift+drag moves full grid. Alt+drag scales full grid. Drag corner handles for anchored resize. Ctrl = fine snap. Wheel zooms. Middle-drag pans.
