Cell border editor

Extract sprites, then click Edit on a sprite. h j k l nudge borders, H J K L coarse nudge, [ and ] change selected edge, arrows move between sprites.
Focused sprite border edit
Use [/] to choose left/top/right/bottom edge. Use h j k l for 1px edits and H J K L for 8px edits. Keys shrink or expand the selected edge based on direction. Exports use the edited bounds.
